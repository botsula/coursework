What can you do with this app?

You can create deadline-tasks of two types: usual (when you have to complete something due special date) and money (when you need to keep some money amount due some date).
This app will make new task in a "Remindozaur" task list on yours Google Tasks. And the main feature is that is is going to update every day so you will be able to see how many days before deadline and how much money you need to keep every day.

How to use it?

If you 'complete' task once a day in your Google Tasks, that means that you made the daily dose of this task. If you did not, in case of 'money' type of task, the next day you would have to keep double dose of money. All information is showed in tasks notes.

How to delete deadline?

If you want to delete the deadline, you just need to delete it from your list in Google Tasks instead of 'complete' it.
